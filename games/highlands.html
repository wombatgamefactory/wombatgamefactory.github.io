Highlands

Signed

Co Designed with Juan Hernández Sánchez. A card-driven engine building euro game set in the Scottish highlands.

Players 2–4
Play Time 90–120 Min
Age 14+
Status Signed — In Production

In Highlands, players take on the role of dedicated conservationists working to repair and rewild the wilderness of Scotland. Using card-driven engine-building and an elegant action selection mechanism, players restore terrain, introduce native flora and fauna, and conduct field research to create a thriving ecosystem.

Highlands will is part of Alley Cat Games advanced games line, which include titles such as Ada's Dream, Baghdad and Calimala.
