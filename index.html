About the Designer

Dean Morris

I'm an Australian board game designer, living in London, who loves pairing clean mechanics with a great physical presence. I spend a lot of time tinkering with prototypes to ensure the actual gameplay feels as good as the game looks. I'm currently working on a few new ideas and am always keen to connect with publishing partners for a chat.
